Territories are no longer limited to a ZIP code

We map out polygons of your routes using the latest in GIS technology, creating a custom waste hauling e-commerce shopping cart for your business.

Our secret sauce

WasteMate views your service territory through the lens of geospatial polygons and is able to determine which services, routes, and prices are appropriate for every single address—all in milliseconds.

A shopping cart built for waste

Because haulers have such a dense amount of data to work with on a daily basis, providing accurate territory pricing online has never been feasible. Truly the first of its kind, WasteMate presents the solution to the problem by creating a shopping cart built for waste.

GIS for humans

WasteMate’s GIS team will help convert your existing data into a geospatial dataset. An experienced team will analyze your service territory and build out polygons for each of your routes and all of your pricing zones. Under the hood, WasteMate uses this data to determine what services and prices are available to each of the addresses in your territory.
