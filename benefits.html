Learn More Streamlining the CSR workflow

WasteMate organizes your company’s information intuitively, saving company resources and resulting in a consistent customer experience.

Service intelligence made simple

With WasteMate Customer Service Representatives no longer have to juggle binders or information about your services and territory. All they need is an address from the customer and WasteMate will provide them with all other details about that address. Which services are available, which day(s) recurring services happen, how much it will cost and how soon you can do it. Day one, new CSRs are able to contribute to happy customers. The CSR job is a lot less stressful and demanding with WasteMate. Management no longer needs to worry about their most knowledgeable CSR being on vacation or retiring. WasteMate empowers your complete team to be experts.

Eliminate the learning curve

WasteMate’s intuitive interface allows anybody to provide customer service, regardless of their background. Haulers can scale up their workforce rapidly, without loss of quality in their CSRs, because of the minimal time required to learn and understand the software. At it’s foundation, WasteMate is designed to help outsiders order and manage waste services on their own. Your employees will be at home in WasteMate the first day they use it.

Elevating your customer experience

By giving your customers the ability to order services online, your business is open and available to them 24/7. CSRs become readily available to deal with customer service issues. Human error is minimized, lines of communication are opened, and all is right in the world.

STEP 1

Customer Order

Customer submits order online from your company’s branded version of WasteMate.

STEP 2

Order Configured

Your CSR receives the order and configures it into office software, confirming delivery.

STEP 3

Confirmation Sent

Customer receives confirmation email with accurate delivery date and is in direct communication with a CSR to answer questions.
