WasteMate works with or without EnCORE®

Regardless of your office management software, WasteMate can benefit your business.

OPTION 1

Compatible with EnCORE®

EnCORE® is a back office software package that is built for the way you work. EnCORE® makes it easy to bill your customers, manage routes, and track productivity.

WasteMate’s partnership with EnCORE® means you’ll never have to enter data twice. Using EnCORE’s API, WasteMate automatically creates new customer accounts, service orders to deliver and remove equipment, adds pricing entries, links routing to pricing, and much more.

If you are an EnCORE® customer, we’re certain we can improve the way you work. WasteMate gives your mobile sales team and in office customer service representatives a whole new way to interact with your back office software.

OPTION 2

Complements your existing office management software

There are numerous options available to haulers for back office software, and we didn’t want to limit WasteMate to just one. WasteMate speaks the language of new accounts, reordering and delivery. It collects data and organizes it, allowing you to update your software, whatever it may be. WasteMate generates visuals and metrics like no other software does. It allows you to see your organization’s potential from a different perspective and consequently plan your operations based on what’s coming down the pipeline.

WasteMate complements any software, including, but not limited to:

Salesforce® Quickbooks® Microsoft Dynamics®
Zapier® Xero® FreshBooks®
